ABOUT US

Every cup tells a story & Every bite is a delight.

At Solstice Café, we believe in the power of a perfect brew to brighten your day. Our beans are sourced from the finest coffee estates around the world, ensuring each sip is a journey through flavor and aroma. Whether you prefer a velvety latte, a robust espresso, or a refreshing cold brew, our skilled baristas craft each cup with passion and precision.

Whether you're a regular or a first-time visitor, we invite you to experience the magic of Solstice Café. Join us for your morning ritual, a lunchtime treat, or an afternoon pick-me-up.
